# SwiftRide NYC Market Entry Playbook — Storyboard

## Audience & Density

- **Audience:** Maya Chen, VP of Operations at SwiftRide
- **Density:** Speaker-led (Maya will present live, not read async)
- **Pattern:** Decision Memo
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, data-forward — no decorative elements

## Core Business Question

Where should SwiftRide deploy its first 50 NYC drivers, when should they work, and how should they position against incumbents?

## One-Sentence Answer

Deploy 50 drivers across 5 zones (JFK Airport, Midtown Center, Times Square, Upper East Side, LaGuardia) with split weekday/weekend shifts, targeting 1-3 mile urban hops and airport premium runs.
